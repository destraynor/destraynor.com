Speaking

I give talks about product, startups, marketing, onboarding, scaling a business and a few other topics. I only speak about things I've worked on and only speak from experience having worked on them while scaling Intercom. In some conference circles this makes me kinda rare in that I'm speaking about how I do my actual job in my actual company where I stand to win or lose based on my performance vs. the more typical circuit-speaker who gives far more polished thoughts about how other people built other companies and what it might mean for the audience.
